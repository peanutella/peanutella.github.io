About Us

"Peanutella is consumable in everyday snacks as it can be partnered to breads."

PEANUTELLA is the combination of peanut butter and chocolate tablea made manually by the members of the enterprise. The difference between this product and the common peanut butter is the upgraded taste from the ingredient cacao and that the oil is reduced and replaced by margarine and natural cacao butter from the seeds of cacao pods.

Our Product

" Cacao is a good source of antioxidants and minerals while peanuts are good for the heart and dieting."

PEANUTELLA is also a substitute to the well-known Nutella, which is a chocolate spread, but in a low price. PEANUTELLA can be bought from a starting price of 60 pesos in a 250 ml bottle. The product is primarily accessible at MTV Lobby of Naga College Foundation and can also contact the enterprise by liking the Facebook page PEANUTELLA @LoJeRoAnVa. The management takes orders and delivers product for free if purchased in bulk orders.

Mission

The enterprise seeks to generate top quality food with one hundred percent customer satisfaction in an affordable price that can compete to existing businesses and products that currently dominates the market. Providing reliable and warm management, Brunchnack Enterprises will treat their employees with utmost respect and will remember to always have fun.

Vision

The management envisions that PEANUTELLA is a profit generating product that has its own production and selling place that will be available in every store to offer its unique taste to the entire Naga City. In addition, Brunchnack Enterprise will be expecting the expansion of its popularity and will be a product of worry-free concept way of eating.
